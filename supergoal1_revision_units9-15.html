📐 Grammar – Simple Present

Use the Simple Present to talk about jobs, habits, and facts.
He / She / It → add -s or -es
✅ I work in a hospital.  |  She teaches English.  |  He drives a bus.
Questions: Do / Does + subject + verb?
Negatives: don't / doesn't + verb
✅ Does he work in a school? – Yes, he does. / No, he doesn't.

📐 Grammar – Telling Time + Prepositions

at + time → at 7 o'clock, at noon
in + morning / afternoon / evening
on + day → on Monday
✅ I wake up at 6:00 in the morning.  |  She does homework in the afternoon.
Telling time: It's + time
3:00 = three o'clock  |  3:15 = quarter past three  |  3:30 = half past three  |  3:45 = quarter to four

📐 Grammar – Modal: can / can't

Use can for ability and possibility.
can does NOT change with he/she/it.
Negative: can't (cannot)
✅ You can swim at the pool.  |  She can't park here.
✅ Can we play football here? – Yes, you can. / No, you can't.

📐 Grammar – be going to (Future)

Subject + am/is/are + going to + verb
Use for planned future actions.
✅ I am going to wear a jacket tomorrow.
✅ She is going to buy new shoes.
✅ Are they going to wear uniforms? – Yes, they are.
💡

Quick tip: am → I  |  is → he/she/it  |  are → you/we/they

📐 Grammar – Present Progressive

Subject + am/is/are + verb-ing
Use for actions happening right now.
✅ They are dancing at the party.
✅ She is blowing out the candles.
✅ Is he singing? – Yes, he is. / No, he isn't.
Spelling rules for -ing:
• dance → dancing (drop e)
• run → running (double consonant)
• play → playing (just add -ing)

Unit 15

Then and Now

Past vs. Present · Simple Past (was/were)

📝 Key Vocabulary – Time & Change

agoمنذ

in the pastفي الماضي

modernحديث
old-fashionedقديم الطراز
technologyتكنولوجيا
transportationمواصلات
communicationتواصل

📐 Grammar – Simple Past: was / were

I / He / She / It → was
You / We / They → were
Negative: wasn't / weren't
✅ Life was different 100 years ago.
✅ There were no computers in 1900.
✅ Was it hot yesterday? – Yes, it was. / No, it wasn't.
used to = something that happened regularly in the past but not now.
Subject + used to + base verb
✅ People used to travel by horse.  |  We used to write letters.
